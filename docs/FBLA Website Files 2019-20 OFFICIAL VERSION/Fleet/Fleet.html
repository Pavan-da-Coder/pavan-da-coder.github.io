Southern Fleet

Southern Air has a medium fleet size, but we are a starting airline and are looking forward to expand our fleet to meet customer demands.

We currently have 4 airplanes in development,
an Airbus A320neo, a Boeing 747-400, a Boeing 777-300ER,
and an Airbus A330-800.



At Southern Air, we currently have 500 aircraft in our fleet.
We have one Airbus A330-900, as well as an Airbus A380-800.
We also have one freighter aircraft, an A330-200F air freighter
for baggage handling. Not only that, but we currently have one
Boeing 737 Max and one helicopter, an Airbus H155 helicopter
for our VIP guests. On top of this, we have numerous pilots
operating small aircraft such as the Cessna 208 Caravan
and the Beechcraft Bonanza. This way we can run daily flights
every morning and afternoon to and from all of our destinations.



We always keep our planes
neat and ready for the next group of passengers,
as well as maintain VIP lounges at airports in
select locations for
passengers that are willing
to fly in style!



We always handle our passenger's baggage
and personal belongings with the utmost respect and caution, and
security and safety measures to prevent the destruction of
personal property.
